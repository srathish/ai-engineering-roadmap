OrbitNote Frequently Asked Questions

What is OrbitNote?
OrbitNote is a note-taking app for researchers. It links your notes into a
graph so that related ideas surface automatically. It is a fictional product
used here as sample data for a Q&A bot.

How much does OrbitNote cost?
OrbitNote has a free tier that allows up to 500 notes. The Pro plan is 8
dollars per month and removes the note limit, adds full-text search, and
enables sharing. The Team plan is 12 dollars per user per month and adds
shared workspaces and admin controls.

Does OrbitNote work offline?
Yes. OrbitNote stores all notes locally first and syncs to the cloud when a
connection is available. You can read and edit every note while offline; sync
resumes automatically once you reconnect.

How do I export my notes?
You can export your entire vault as Markdown files or as a single JSON bundle
from Settings, under Data and Export. Exports include your note links so the
graph can be rebuilt elsewhere. There is no lock-in.

Is my data private?
Notes are encrypted in transit and at rest. On the Pro and Team plans you can
enable end-to-end encryption, after which OrbitNote staff cannot read your
note contents even on the server.
