OrbitNote Support Notes

Resetting your password:
Go to the sign-in screen and choose "Forgot password". You will receive an
email with a reset link that is valid for thirty minutes. If the email does
not arrive, check your spam folder and confirm you used the address tied to
your account.

Syncing problems:
If a device is not syncing, first confirm it is online. Then open Settings,
Sync, and tap "Force sync". If a conflict is detected, OrbitNote keeps both
versions of the note and marks the newer one as primary so nothing is lost.

Recovering a deleted note:
Deleted notes move to Trash and stay there for thirty days before they are
permanently removed. To recover one, open Trash from the sidebar, select the
note, and choose Restore. After thirty days recovery is not possible.

Keyboard shortcuts:
Press Cmd-K (or Ctrl-K on Windows) to open the quick switcher and jump to any
note by name. Press Cmd-L to insert a link to another note. Press Cmd-Shift-F
to run a full-text search across the whole vault, which requires a Pro or Team
plan.

Contacting support:
Pro and Team customers get priority email support with a one business day
response target. Free tier users can use the community forum, which is
monitored by the OrbitNote team.
