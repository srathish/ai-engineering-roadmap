# Team

The team has 6 engineers and 2 designers spread across two time zones (Berlin and
San Francisco). We run two-week sprints and hold sprint planning every other
Monday.

On-call rotation covers business hours in both time zones. Priya leads the
backend group; Marcus leads the frontend group. Design is led by Sofia.

New engineers go through a one-week onboarding covering the codebase, the
deployment pipeline, and our incident-response runbook.
